About ViTA

ViTA ("Visualiza tu Aprendizaje") is a web application that support learners in keeping track of their knowledge in courses. Features: - Instructor can easily define course structures and share these with the learners - Learners can define their own level of expertise in the course topics - Learners can also label the difficulty levels of the topics - ViTA shows aggregated levels of expertise and difficulty of the topics of the course
